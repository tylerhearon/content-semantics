Beetroot (Beta Vulgaris)

  1. Common Varieties
  2. Nutritional Information
  3. Tags

55 to 65 days are needed from germinatioin to harvest of the root.

The usually deep purple roots of the beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a sald after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegatable.
